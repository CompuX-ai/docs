## Compute Credit Pricing Models

Understanding the different pricing models for compute credits is essential for optimizing costs. The most common models include pay-as-you-go, reserved capacity, and spot instances. Pay-as-you-go offers the most flexibility, charging you only for the resources you consume. Reserved capacity provides large discounts for committing to long-term usage. Spot instances offer even deeper discounts but come with the risk of interruption. Each model has its trade-offs, and the best choice depends on your specific workload and usage patterns.
